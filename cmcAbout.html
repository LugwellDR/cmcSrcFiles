About

The "Clean My Campus" mobile application allows students and faculty to take ownership of their campus and keeping it clean. Through the main GUI, users can report cleanliness or maintenance problems or take charge of cleaning an area themselves. Through the admin portal, users can view reports, traffic data, and more.



This project's sponsors are Brock Brothers and Ellen Vaughan. Brock Brothers is a supervisor and coordinator for NAU's Moving and Recycling Department. Ellen Vaughan is a former manager for the NAU Office of Sustainability, and currently at UC Santa Cruz.



The "Clean My Campus" Mobile Application & Web Portal were developed by team MoGreen as a Capstone project. Our team website and more information about development are here.
